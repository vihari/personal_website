Bspoke Facebook app privacy policy

Introduction

Bspoke app is solely for research purpose and does not use your data for commercial purposes. We are committed to keep your data discreet and use it solely for research purpose.


By using the app you agree to observe and be bound by this Privacy Policy and you consent to the fetch of your information from facebook in accordance with the terms. Please do not use the App if you do not agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information we collect

If you choose to use this app, it will collect your Facebook posts, Facebook pages that you have liked, your facebook friend lists(like close friends, family e.t.c.), various checkins and various places you were tagged in. Bspoke app generally only mines posts that are a given number of days old as set by the user through the Bspoke software settings page.

How we use and disclose the information

Any personal data fetched from facebook will not be communicated to any server but shall retain to the user's computer, which then can be used by Bspoke software to profile the user. It is upon user to keep the data discreet and one can clear the data whenever he wishes to.

By using the App and agreeing to this Privacy Policy, users authorise us to collect non-personal statistics like number of posts, number of checked in places, number of friends and number of page liked. We will use such statistics for research purpose but still keeping the anonymity of the user.

Changes to this Privacy Policy; Contacting Us

You agree that Bspoke may change this Privacy Policy at any time. Our current Privacy Policy will be posted here: http://vihari.github.io/personal_website/others/privacy-policy.html. Our contact email for questions is: hangal@cs.stanford.edu.
